## Why it remains available

The collection documents an earlier phase of KiWiS as a student-facing setup wiki. The original MDX files, screenshots, and instructions remain in the repository under `legacy-content`. Existing `/docs`, `/appendix`, and `/faq` links now lead here so outdated instructions are not presented as current guidance.
