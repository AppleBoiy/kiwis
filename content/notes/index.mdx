## Resolved stellar populations

The current astronomy work uses resolved stars as tracers of low-surface-brightness structure around nearby galaxies. The record focuses on three questions:

- How were overlapping HST detections removed without distorting the spatial distribution?
- Which background model is appropriate for each galaxy environment?
- Does a candidate feature remain significant under Poisson counting uncertainty?

## Ontology engineering

The ontology track documents how domain concepts become explicit classes, properties, constraints, and inference rules. Current work includes an ontology-augmented health-service recommendation system with SWRL reasoning and SPARQL-based inspection.

The durable lesson is architectural: a recommendation should remain traceable to modeled domain knowledge rather than only to an opaque score.

## Knowledge graphs

The graph track connects papers, projects, datasets, methods, and technical dependencies. Provenance is treated as part of the data model: every relationship should retain enough context to answer where it came from and why it exists.

## Note lifecycle

1. Capture a question or observation.
2. Link the relevant source, dataset, or figure.
3. Record the method and assumptions.
4. Promote stable explanations into a method ledger or glossary entry.
5. Preserve superseded material when it remains historically useful.
