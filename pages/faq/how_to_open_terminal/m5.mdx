## Method 5: Using the Go Menu

- Open a new Finder window.
- In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click on `Go`

<img
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/CSCMU-65s/cs-wiki101/main/img/how_to_open_Terminal/go-menu.jpeg"
  alt="Go Menu"
/>

- From the dropdown menu, select `Utilities`.

  <img
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/CSCMU-65s/cs-wiki101/main/img/how_to_open_Terminal/utilities.jpeg"
    alt="Utilities"
  />

- Scroll down or search for the `Terminal` application and double-click on it.

  <img
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/CSCMU-65s/cs-wiki101/main/img/how_to_open_Terminal/Terminal_in_utilities.jpeg"
    alt="Terminal in Utilities"
  />
